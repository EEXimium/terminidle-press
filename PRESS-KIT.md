## Description

TERMINIDLE starts you with nothing. Not "few resources" nothing — actual
nothing: **no mouse cursor** (that's an upgrade), **no sound** (speakers are
hardware you buy), **no windows** (comfort is earned). You type `mine`, one
byte arrives, and the whole game grows out of that moment.

The early hours are pure terminal: build a botnet, merge bots into server
clusters, fill a 43-node skill tree, take typed hacking contracts where speed
and accuracy multiply the payout, play the crypto market, and repel rival
hackers breaching your firewall. Later, the PROCESS MANAGER turns your data
rate into an active game of heat management — buy real components for the RIG
BAY, overclock them for throughput, and watch a part **literally burn out**
if you ride the redline too long.

Then comes the ascension: buy the DESKTOP ENVIRONMENT and the terminal
shrinks into a window on a full graphical OS — taskbar, start menu, draggable
windows, desktop widgets, your own wallpaper. Everything you built becomes an
app. New ones appear: arcade classics (SNAKE, PONG, MINESWEEPER) that pay
real data, an inbox with typed work orders, scams, and one very persistent
Nigerian prince, a darknet hub with a chat full of suspiciously human bots
(the game is honest about it), rotating black-market deals from sellers whose
negative REP is a warning you'll ignore exactly once, and a live map of the
internet to capture node by node, all the way to the QUANTUM CORE.

Finally, the QUANTUM LAB: ascend and trigger a **total system wipe** — every
upgrade, every item, the OS itself — traded for Quantum Bits and ten
permanent upgrades. Arm PROTOCOLS (self-imposed challenge constraints: no
clusters, no automation, no hacks, no consumables) and honor them to
ascension for multiplied payouts. Only your trophies, records, and darknet
reputation survive the reset. Everything else, you get to build again —
faster, and by choice.

## Features

- **Everything is earned** — the mouse cursor, audio, windows, even copy-paste comforts are upgrades. The game begins 100% keyboard.
- **A real command line**: 40+ commands, tab completion with argument awareness, in-game man pages. Every window is optional.
- **A 43-node skill tree** where each purchase reveals the next node — five branches across mining, botnet, hacking, crypto, and interface.
- **Typed hack contracts**: a quest chain of break-ins where typing the exploit fast and clean multiplies the reward.
- **RIG BAY overclocking**: buy CPU/GPU/PSU/cooler parts, push the dial, manage heat — redline too long and a component burns for good until you replace it.
- **TERMINIDLE OS**: the incremental game that boots its own desktop — taskbar, start menu, right-click context menus, buyable live widgets, custom wallpapers.
- **Arcade apps that pay**: SNAKE, PONG, MINESWEEPER, and a disk-defrag minigame, all wired into the real economy.
- **A darknet with personality**: simulated peer chat that reacts to your milestones, a black market with deliberately shady sellers, real-time crew heists that resolve even while the game is closed, and an ascension leaderboard.
- **NETWORK TAKEOVER**: spread your botnet across a live map of the internet, from your LAN switch to the QUANTUM CORE.
- **Rogue packets, mining combos, personal-best records** — constant reasons to touch the keyboard.
- **PROTOCOLS**: challenge-run constraints that multiply prestige payouts. Your run becomes a build decision.
- **Total-wipe prestige**: ascension takes literally everything except the quantum layer, your trophies, and your reputation.
- **Offline progress** up to 24 hours, an always-filling goal tracker, and a "flux stream" that visualizes your income's speed and size in real time.
- **46+ trophies**, each a permanent +1% — and a few secrets.
