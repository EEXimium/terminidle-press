Description

TERMINIDLE starts you with nothing. Not "few resources" nothing — actual nothing: no mouse cursor (that's an upgrade), no sound (speakers are hardware you buy), no windows (comfort is earned). You type mine, one byte arrives, and the whole game grows out of that moment.

The early hours are pure terminal: build a botnet, merge bots into server clusters, fill a 43-node skill tree, take typed hacking contracts where speed and accuracy multiply the payout, play the crypto market, and repel rival hackers breaching your firewall. Later, the PROCESS MANAGER turns your data rate into an active game of heat management — buy real components for the RIG BAY, overclock them for throughput, and watch a part literally burn out if you ride the redline too long.

Then comes the ascension: buy the DESKTOP ENVIRONMENT and the terminal shrinks into a window on a full graphical OS — taskbar, start menu, draggable windows, desktop widgets, your own wallpaper. Everything you built becomes an app, and new ones appear: arcade classics (SNAKE, PONG, MINESWEEPER) that pay real data, an inbox with typed work orders, scams, and one very persistent Nigerian prince, a darknet hub with a chat full of suspiciously human bots (the game is honest about it), black-market deals from sellers whose negative REP is a warning you'll ignore exactly once, and a live map of the internet to capture node by node, all the way to the QUANTUM CORE.

Finally, the QUANTUM LAB: ascend and trigger a total system wipe — every upgrade, every item, the OS itself — traded for Quantum Bits and ten permanent upgrades. Arm PROTOCOLS (self-imposed constraints: no clusters, no automation, no hacks, no consumables) and honor them to ascension for multiplied payouts. Only your trophies, records, and darknet reputation survive. Everything else, you get to build again — faster, and by choice.

Features

  • Everything is earned — the mouse cursor, audio, and windows are upgrades. The game begins 100% keyboard.
  • A real command line: 40+ commands, context-aware tab completion, in-game man pages. Every window is optional.
  • A 43-node skill tree where each purchase reveals the next node, across mining, botnet, hacking, crypto and interface branches.
  • Typed hack contracts — break in fast and clean for multiplied rewards.
  • RIG BAY overclocking: buy CPU/GPU/PSU/cooler parts, push the dial, manage heat. Redline too long and a component burns until you replace it.
  • TERMINIDLE OS — the idle game that boots its own desktop: taskbar, start menu, right-click menus, buyable live widgets, custom wallpaper.
  • Arcade apps that pay: SNAKE, PONG, MINESWEEPER and disk-defrag, wired into the real economy.
  • A darknet with personality: simulated peer chat that reacts to your milestones, shady market sellers, real-time crew heists that resolve while the game is closed, an ascension leaderboard.
  • NETWORK TAKEOVER: capture a live map of the internet, from your LAN switch to the QUANTUM CORE.
  • Rogue packets, mining combos, personal-best records — constant reasons to touch the keyboard.
  • PROTOCOLS: challenge-run constraints that multiply prestige payouts — every run is a build decision.
  • Total-wipe prestige: ascension takes literally everything except the quantum layer, your trophies and your REP.
  • Offline progress up to 24h, an always-filling goal tracker, and a flux stream visualizing income speed and size in real time.
  • 46+ trophies, each a permanent +1% — a few of them secret.
